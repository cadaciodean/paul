ABOUT RMS ELECTRO

RMS ELECTRO is a small but with Integrity Electrical Maintenance Services, we provide technicians maintain, repair, install, and upgrade electrical and mechanical equipment, components, tools, and machinery for your home, commercial or industrial business,hotels,factories,airports,apartment complexes and warehouses.

RMS ELECTROS'S RESPONSIBILITIES are to troubleshoot,perform tests,cost analysis report and to follow safety regulations regarding our duties.
We provide a variety of electrical installation options and our expert team of electricians work to the highest of standards.


We also provide our customers with regular preventive maintenance services. Our electricians conduct a series of checks and tests within a property, making recommendations and avoiding any unnecessary downtime.

RMS ELECTRO is not all about profits we value safety so our customers can relax and have a peace of mind. Aside from that ten percent of our earnings goes to charities that are in needs. RMS ELECTRO belives that everyone should be responsible to one another.
